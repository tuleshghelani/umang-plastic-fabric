Our Story

From humble beginnings to green net industry leadership

Building Excellence Since 2021

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the green net manufacturing industry, Umang Plastic Fabric began as a small family-owned business in Gujarat, India. Our founder, Mr. Harshil Sagapariya, started with a simple mission: to create high-quality green nets and shade nets that would serve agricultural and commercial needs while maintaining environmental responsibility.

Over the years, we've grown from a local manufacturer to a trusted partner for businesses across multiple sectors. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainability has remained unwavering, driving us to continuously improve our processes and expand our product portfolio.

Today, we're proud to be recognized as one of Gujarat's leading green net manufacturers, serving diverse industries including agriculture, construction, residential, and commercial sectors with our premium ROSE brand products.
