Our Premium Shade Net Manufacturing Process

Built on precision engineering and strict quality control to deliver durable, high-performance shade nets for agriculture, commercial, and residential applications.

From Material Selection to Finishing

At Umang Plastic Fabric, we follow a carefully designed, proven workflow to achieve consistent quality and long-lasting performance. Each stage is optimized for strength, UV stability, and uniform mesh construction, ensuring our nets look premium and perform reliably across seasons.

  1. HD T9 granules selection

    We begin with high-grade HD T9 granules to ensure superior tensile strength, color consistency, and longevity. Raw material integrity is foundational to premium output.

  2. Film roll extrusion

    We extrude the granules into uniform film rolls (also referred to as guaranillas) with tight thickness control, optimizing for durability and UV resistance.

  3. Precision slitting to 1.5 mm thread

    The film roll is loaded onto a slitting machine and cut to produce 1.5 mm thread. The slit thread is passed through a heater to stabilize dimensions and reduce shrinkage.

  4. Net weaving

    The 1.5 mm thread is precisely fed into our net machines, forming a uniform mesh. This step defines the shade percentage, airflow, and overall mechanical strength.

  5. Folding and finishing

    Finished shade net is loaded onto the folding machine for clean, compact folds. We follow stringent finishing checks for edge consistency, roll density, and packaging quality.

  6. Quality assurance & dispatch

    Every batch undergoes QA checks for strength, UV stability, and dimensional accuracy before dispatch. This ensures a premium, reliable product for our customers.
